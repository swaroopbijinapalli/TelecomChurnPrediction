## 📊 Telecom Churn Prediction 

### **Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing telecom customer data and building machine learning models to identify customers who are likely to churn. The goal is to help telecom companies retain high-value customers by predicting churn risk early and identifying key factors influencing customer behavior. ([GitHub][1])

---

### **Business Problem**

The telecom industry is highly competitive, and customers frequently switch service providers. Retaining existing customers is more cost-effective than acquiring new ones, making churn prediction a critical business objective. ([GitHub][1])

The project aims to:

* Analyze customer-level telecom data
* Predict customers at high risk of churn
* Identify major drivers influencing churn behavior

---

### **Churn Definition**

The project explains how churn differs between prepaid and postpaid customers.

* **Postpaid:** churn is clear when a customer cancels service
* **Prepaid:** churn is harder to detect because users may stop using services without notice

This makes churn prediction especially important in markets like India where prepaid usage is dominant. ([GitHub][1])

---

### **Project Methodology**

The project analyzes telecom data across multiple months to detect behavioral patterns.

Time window approach:

* First months → normal customer behavior
* Next phase → potential churn signals
* Final phase → churn outcome

Models are trained using earlier data to predict future churn. ([Ecosyste.ms][2])

### **Objective**

Build predictive models that:
✔ Identify high-risk customers
✔ Detect churn indicators
✔ Support customer retention strategies
